Meet the Founder

Meet Oyin.

Oyindamola (Oyin) Babatunde is an entrepreneur, marketing strategist, and community builder helping women create wealth, opportunity, and meaningful lives in the diaspora.

With nearly a decade of experience in digital marketing, SaaS, and entrepreneurship, she has helped businesses generate significant revenue through strategic growth, paid media, and digital systems. Her work sits at the intersection of community, entrepreneurship, and culture — making her voice uniquely relatable to modern immigrant families navigating life abroad.
